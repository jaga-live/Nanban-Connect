About Nanban

Nanban is a A to Z Student Services Company for Students, Colleges, Decision Makers and Education companies.
Think Education! Think Nanban!
We are working towards the United Nations SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals)
especially SDG 4: Quality Education and SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth



OUR MISSION

Our goal is to be the World's Largest Marketplace to fulfill all requirements of Students and Colleges across the world.

OUR VISION

Nanban came into existence because we want to solve Education and Employment across the world.




FOR STUDENTS



Students can approach Nanban to solve all their problems. Nanban is a one stop company for all Student services. We are trying to build and connect India's Largest Student network across 3500+ college campuses. A student can approach Nanban for services like Scholarships, E-Learning, Skill Development, Loans, Innovation grants, student discounts, Internships and Placements etc



FOR COLLEGES



College Campuses can approach Nanban for services like Lab establishments, Technology development, Campus Infrastructure Management, Admissions, Funding, Research, Event Management, Resource Management etc. We are trying to build and connect the India's Largest Student network across 3500+ college campuses which enable us to serve premium services at an affordable cost.

FOR COMPANIES



Companies who work in the Education domain can approach Nanban to promote their products and services across our network. We are a A to Z Student services company including E-Learning, Training, Discounts, Research, Infrastructure Management, Technology services etc. We are trying to build and connect the India's Largest Student network across 3500+ college campuses which will enable us to attain 50% market reach
